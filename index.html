About Dhoni
Mahendra Singh Dhoni, commonly known as M.S. Dhoni, is a former Indian international cricketer and one of the most successful cricket captains in the world.
Dhoni is known for his calm and composed demeanor on the field, exceptional leadership skills, and remarkable finishing abilities as a batsman. He led the Indian cricket team to numerous victories, including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup.
Post his retirement from international cricket, Dhoni continues to inspire millions with his contribution to the sport and his various business ventures.
Achievements
  • 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup:
    • Led the Indian cricket team to victory in the ICC Cricket World Cup held in 2011.
    • Showcased exceptional captaincy and finishing skills in the final against Sri Lanka.
  • 2007 ICC World Twenty20:
    • Captained the team to win the inaugural ICC World Twenty20 in 2007.
    • Dhoni's innovative captaincy and calm demeanor were crucial in the team's success.
  • Padma Bhushan:
    • Awarded the Padma Bhushan, the third-highest civilian award in the Republic of India, in 2018.
    • Recognized for his outstanding contribution to Indian cricket.
